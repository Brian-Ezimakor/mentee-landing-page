About the Program

The inspire Her Afrika Mentorship program is a 4-month (July-october) program with a one month bootcamp, designed to connect early-career African women with experienced mentors. This program aims to provide guidance, and a platform for skill development, fostering a community of growth and empowerment.

The Inspire Her Afrika Mentorship Program is a transformative initiative designed to empower early-career women (0-3 years of work experience) with key skills and mentorship opportunities to excel in their careers. it comprises two phases: a Bootcamp phase and a Main Mentorship phase.
The Bootcamp phase runs for one month, after which successful participants transition to the Main Mentorship phase for 3 months. The detailed timeline for the mentorship phase will be communicated to participants.
The program aims to build the capacity of African women by providing practical skills, networking opportunities and guidance to thrive in chosen career paths.
